// CASE STUDY

The $0 YouTube Shorts Factory

How we built a daily AI-powered content engine — at zero operating cost.

The Problem

Red River AI needed consistent video content to build brand presence — but creating Shorts manually was slow, expensive, and inconsistent. Hiring editors, writing scripts, recording voiceovers, and rendering videos took hours per Short. At scale, that's unsustainable.

The Solution

A fully automated video pipeline running on a ThinkPad T490s in Winnipeg. RSS feeds scrape AI news daily at 6:30 AM. Gemini Flash writes 50-70 word scripts. ElevenLabs generates voiceovers. FFmpeg composites the branded Short — then n8n uploads it to YouTube, X, and Facebook. All automated. All zero cost.

Tech Stack

  • n8n — workflow orchestration & scheduling
  • OpenRouter — Gemini Flash via LLM API
  • ElevenLabs — AI voice synthesis
  • FFmpeg — branded video compositing
  • FastAPI — local render service
  • YouTube / X / Facebook — multi-platform publish
